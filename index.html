A look inside my life.

Welcome to my Project 0 Personal Website. This website will give you a little bit of an insight in my very busy life. I hope you enjoy navigating through a little bit of information about me, my personal goals, each of my children, and also enjoy the pictures that I have. I love capturing moments even when they are not of an actual object or person. I like the idea of “living in the moment” therefore, most of my pictures are generally from any place, local or out of town. Although time is limited, I try to expose my children to different events as much as I can so that way they are able to explore more now that they are curious little bees. I used to do blogs so I found it only necessary that I include that on my personal website as well. Although, I am less likely to put more information out there than I was when I was a teenager blogging away, I hope to put it in enough words to give off the same type of vibe. I enjoy writing and keeping users interested in my stories without giving too much details. I have enjoyed this class very much as it allowed me to be creative.
